Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 aims for innovation, digital services, startups, and inclusive tech-driven growth.

Sustainability

In Bangladesh 2.0, sustainability means using technology for green growth, equality, and resilience.

Infrastructure

In new Bangladesh, infrastructure focuses on smart cities, digital connectivity, and green development.

Empowerment

In Bangladesh 2.0, empowerment means enabling youth and citizens through innovation and technology.
